About Canopy Corp

We are an upcoming battery manufacturing company focused on sustainability and efficiency. Our manufacturing operations will also include recycling previously used batteries to reduce environmental waste. Founded by Indrajeet Date a post-graduate from the Department of Sustainable Energy Engineering at IIT Kanpur, Canopy Corp is committed to clean energy innovation.

Our Technology Focus

Recyclable Battery Systems

We are developing battery systems designed with end-of-life recycling in mind, reducing waste and environmental impact.

Advanced Lithium-Ion Alternatives

Exploring alternatives to traditional lithium-ion cells that are safer, longer-lasting, and resource-efficient.

SIB and LIB Dual Chemistry Platform

“R&D-driven development of both lithium- and sodium-based chemistries to balance cost, performance, and material availability.”
